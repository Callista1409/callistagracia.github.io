Callista Gracia

Artist | Fiction Writer

Callista is a student at UPH Medan with a deep passion for art. Having loved drawing since childhood, she recently transitioned to digital art in 2023. Callista now runs an e-commerce store selling merchandise featuring her artwork. Her love for reading since childhood also transformed her into a fiction writer on the Wattpad platform in 2020, with three completed works, and has also participated in the FLC project to contribute to an anthology of short stories. Under the pen name Lemonychee, Callista continues to create online content and enthusiastically manages her personal projects.
