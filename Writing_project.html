FLC Project: Sableng School Life Anthology

Callista, under the name Lemonychee, joined Four Leaf Clover, a writing community, in 2020. In 2022, all 35 members collaborated to create an anthology of short stories depicting the daily lives of each member as students at Clover High School (CHS), where each was tasked with writing about themselves alongside four randomly assigned members. Titled "Don't Hide Under Your Incertitude, Can You?", Lemonychee (Callista) explored the theme of the importance of self-confidence. This anthology stands as a testament to the enduring bond among the members of Four Leaf Clover, who will always remember one another.

Nusantara Pen Circle: Daily Writing Challenge 2021

Callista, known by the name Lemonychee, is a member of Nusantara Pen Circle (NPC), a writing community established in 2015. In 2020, she passed a rigorous examination with a graduation rate of only 3.21%. NPC is a diverse community that hosts a variety of exciting events, one of which is the 30 Daily Writing Challenge. This challenge requires members to write every day on themes provided by the moderator. Through "Cerita Si Pengembara," Lemonychee (Callista) weaves abstract themes into a cohesive serialized story, wrapped in a dark fairytale theme that subtly critiques humanity and the ideologies of the world.

Writing Challenge with Polaroid Publisher

"Ameliorate" is a novelette created for a competition hosted by Polaroid Publisher, which was divided into two genres: Fantasy and Young-Adult. Competing in the Young-Adult category, "Ameliorate" addresses the issues faced by teenagers transitioning into adulthood. Through 16 chapters, Lemonychee (Callista) crafts a narrative that explores mental health, as well as symbols of hope and existentialism, through the protagonist's struggle to attain validation.
