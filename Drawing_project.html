My Drawing Projects

Organization Merch Project

As a member of HMPSSI, Callista is responsible for illustrating the HMPSSI mascot named Cody. One of the projects planned by HMPSSI is Cody merchandise. One of the illustrations to be used features Cody relaxing on the beach, which will be made into stickers and tote bags

Frieren: Beyond Journey End Fanart

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End is one of the most memorable series. This fan art was created over the course of more than 13 hours, exploring various coloring and shading techniques inspired by the work of an artist with the username "orangesekaii." Combined with a personal art style, it resulted in a fan art of Frieren, one of the main characters in the series.

Character Design

To complete the "UI/UX Design" course in the second semester, Callista, along with the "Go Go ICB" team, designed a history learning app called "Sydu." A mascot in the form of an owl named "Dudu" was created to enhance the visual appeal of the app and to foster engagement by providing a friendly and approachable interface that encourages users to explore historical content in an interactive way.
